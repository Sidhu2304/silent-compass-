How to Use Silent Compass

Step 1: Connect

Click "Connect Device" and select "Silent Compass" from the Bluetooth list. If you don't have the device, click "Try Demo Mode" to test the app.

Step 2: Navigate

Once connected, use the Turn Left and Turn Right buttons. The device will vibrate on your left or right side to show direction.

Step 3: Stop

Press the Stop button to stop all vibrations.
